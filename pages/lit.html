a psychological drama novel by 

Paul Taylor Miller

"Tomorrow would have been the best day. Tomorrow would have made happy memories. But after death knocked, he sets out to stop more - as he walks on a bitter path to vigilantism."

Brian Wolf receives a mysterious invitation to the United States from an old friend. But after what had happened to his family of years past, Brian hesitated to come. After the long, arduous flight away from old Ireland, he awaits the first sound and sight of all his friends in one place- the University of Pennsylvania. Something felt amiss, but it had been too late to keep death outside. Not long after, headlines break out about Brian's heroic act against death taking more lives. But everyone recognises him now – endangering everyone he knows, destroying him in all fronts – leaving no room for the life once lived.

A new venture by Paul Taylor Miller since the closure of the Poppin Brown music project in September 2023, it is a psychological drama novel in the making since June of that year.

Its open alpha began in 25 January 2024, and is also set for digital and print publication by Draft2Digital LLC later in the same year.
