Poppin Brown

This was the project that properly started my music journey.

It was my academy’s Appreciation Week back in 2022, and in typical Miller fashion (I didn’t call myself that at the time), music was a major component in my presentation for appreciation. Then I thought to release the original music onto streaming platforms – this ended up being reality as my parents agreed to pay for single distribution.

I find it funny that I’ve been making music since 2019 (five years now at the time of writing this), but I’ve only decided to release my work everywhere a full three years later. It wasn’t my best, but it was a start.

Now, my name had been out there – but at the time, I called myself ‘Poppin Brown’. When I look back at it, I’m like… ‘why did I name myself this?’ ‘Why did I think about that?’ The old stage name eventually became the name of the experimental music project I had, to drop the ridiculous name.

Next thing I did was plan out the entire journey ahead, so I decided to go through ‘stages’. Only two were made, though – Discovery, and Renaissance, respectively. This wasn’t sustainable, and in the end I decided to close out the project because of this, on top of lack of motivation and college obligations.

The project is still up if you’d like to listen to it – it's on the top of the page. Though I warn you, it will be a mess. I've since shifted to making music under my current name.
